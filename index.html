☀️ Sun

☀️

The Sun is a star that gives us heat and light. Scientists study it with missions like NASA's Parker Solar Probe to learn more about solar flares and space weather.

☄️ Mercury

☄️

Mercury is the smallest and closest planet to the Sun. Missions like MESSENGER and BepiColombo help scientists understand its surface and temperature changes.

🌕 Venus

🌕

Venus is covered with thick clouds and is very hot. Probes like Magellan and future Indian missions study its surface and atmosphere.

🌍 Earth

🌍

Earth is our home and the only planet known to support life. Satellites monitor its weather, climate, and environment to help protect the planet.

🔴 Mars

🔴

Mars is known as the Red Planet. India's Mangalyaan mission made history by being the first to reach Mars in its first attempt. Scientists explore Mars for signs of past life.

🟡 Jupiter

🟡

Jupiter is the biggest planet in our solar system. NASA's Juno mission studies its storms and huge magnetic field.

🪐 Saturn

🪐

Saturn is famous for its rings. The Cassini spacecraft gave us amazing pictures and data about Saturn and its moons.

🔵 Uranus

🔵

Uranus spins on its side! Scientists use telescopes and Voyager 2 data to study its cold atmosphere and rings.

🌀 Neptune

🌀

Neptune is far away and very windy. It was studied by Voyager 2 and observed using large telescopes to understand its weather and structure.
